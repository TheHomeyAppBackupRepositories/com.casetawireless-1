Caséta is a smart lighting control system by Lutron. Set up around the Caséta Wireless Smart Bridge, the system includes smart switches and dimmers that replace existing wall switches, making ordinary bulbs smart.

The Lutron Caséta App for Homey allows you to connect Homey Pro to the Caséta Smart Bridge. Control, automate and monitor your Caséta lights from Homey, and combine your Caséta devices with the other devices in your smart home.
